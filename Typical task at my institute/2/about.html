About OZSolar Pty Ltd:

We are a 100 % privately owned Australian Company registered in Victoria. OZSolar Pty Ltd is into marketing and installation of solar systems in Australia for more than a decade.
We are member of Clean Energy Council of Australia. CEC is the regulatory authority for the industry that commands what quality panels and inverters can go on Australian properties. Not every solar company is member of CEC. As a customer, you can be well assured that you are dealing with an ethical organization that complies with high standards established by Clean Energy Council of Australia.
